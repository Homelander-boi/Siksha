Frequently Asked Questions

What types of courses does SIKSHA offer?

SIKSHA offers a diverse range of courses across various fields, including technology, business, design, marketing, and personal development. Our platform includes beginner to advanced levels, catering to all learning needs.

How do I enroll in a course on SIKSHA?

To enroll, simply browse our course catalog, select the course you’re interested in, and click the “Enroll” button. Follow the prompts to complete your registration and payment if applicable.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Course prerequisites vary. Each course page provides details about any required background knowledge or skills. Some courses may be suitable for beginners, while others may require prior experience.

Can I access the courses on mobile devices?

The mobile version of SIKSHA is coming very soon...till then happy learning.

How long do I have access to a course once I enroll?

Once enrolled, you typically have lifetime access to the course materials. This means you can revisit the content and complete the course at your own pace.

What payment methods are accepted for in courses?

SIKSHA accepts various payment methods, including major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment options. You can choose the payment method that is most convenient for you during the enrollment process.

What if I have technical issues while using the platform?

If you encounter technical issues, our support team is available to help. You can contact us through the support page, and we will assist you in resolving any problems.

Are there any free courses available on SIKSHA?

Yes, SIKSHA offers a selection of free courses and trial lessons. You can explore these to get a feel for the platform before committing to paid courses.

How do I know the right Course for me?

Our platform features progress tracking tools. You can monitor your course completion, quiz scores, and assignments through your personal dashboard.

Can I interact with instructors and other students?

Absolutely! Many courses offer discussion forums, live Q&A sessions, and peer interaction features. You can engage with instructors and fellow students to enhance your learning experience.
